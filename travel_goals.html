My Travel Goals

Places that I want to travel in my life

Welcome to one of my travel goals. On this website, I want to showcase the best countries to visit. Since I was a child, it is one of my dreams to travel all around the world. That is why the purpose of this website is to organize the places that I want to visit. This list is based on my own ranking, starting from the number 5 to 1.

5. Singapore

My number 5 on the list will be Singapore. The reason why I chose this place as number 5 because it is near my hometown which is the Philippines. What I love about this country is that it has disciplined people of different races all over Asia. Hopefully, if I have enough money I can finally go to this place.

4. Australia

My number 4 will on the list will be Australia. It is rank number 4 in my rankings due to its geographic location which is quite near to the Philippines. I have close relatives in this country which can give me opportunities to travel to that place soon. What I love about this country is its western culture despite it is found in the eastern part of the globe. This country has beautiful places ranging from white beaches to adventurous cities. In the near the future, I would definitely go to this place.

3. Canada

Number 3 will belong to Canada. The reason why I chose Canada as number 3 is because it has a breath-taking view in there. It is balanced with the city and nature in which I definitely like. I also have a close relative in this country which can hopefully allow me to travel there. Even though the ticket price might be expensive, I hopefully pray that I could there.

2. Japan

Bring the drum rolls *drumrolls effect* The number 2 on the places that I want to visit will be japan. Since I was a child, I love to watch anime, read manga, and even watch movies at the cinema. I was in love with Japanese culture. That is why I have decided to travel to Japan if I have enough budget. Even though traveling to Japan might be the most expensive budget among all these top 5 places, the view there will surely be breath-taking. Ranging from tall buildings to the sensational view of sakura trees, Japan will surely bring all things that you want to your bucket list.

1. Switzerland

Finally! For my number 1 place that I want to visit will be Switzerland. Among the other four places, this country has the most beautiful and breath-taking view. This country is one of the ideal retirement vacations that I want to go to. The major reason why I love this place is because of its relaxing green sceneries that nature has to offer. It makes me appreciate the creation that God made. Hopefully in the future, I would travel to this place.
